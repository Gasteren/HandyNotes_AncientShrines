| **Buff Name**       | **Effect**                                                     |
|---------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|

| Ancient Power       | Increases damage dealt by 15% for 1 hour.                      |
| Ancient Winds       | Increases critical strike, haste, and mastery by 5,000 for 1 hour. |
